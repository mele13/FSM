Disabled

Many people think that having a disability will keep you from doing exercise. That is rather not true.
Our gyms features plenty of different equipment and activities fit for everyone no matter the disability.
We also count with meny professionals that can help you excercise and give you some advice. If you are not sure if exercise is safe for you or if you are currently inactive, ask your doctor.

At FSM we have disabled-oriented courses that may be of your inteterest.
