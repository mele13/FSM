Physiotherapy at FSM

Concerned with the prevention, treatment and rehabilitation of patients with muscular and skeletal disorders through various types of interventions, including manual therapy, postural therapy, manipulative therapy and physical therapy.
Physical therapy is becoming increasingly important as part of a tailor-made programme for each patient. Therapeutic exercises helps restoring mental and physical wellbeing and preventing pain, injuries and relapses.

The importance of therapeutic exercise

Treat or prevent aches and pains
Restore proper physical function
Preventing health risk factors
Improving one's general physical condition
Increase not only physical but also psychological well-being
