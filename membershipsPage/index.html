Become a member

To become a member you need to go to the closest gym. We’ll take a photograph for your profile, but don’t worry... there’s a mirror right next to the front desk!
Don’t forget your ID or any identification document. If you’re applying for a special membership, don’t forget the special documentation you may need:

  • Family: ID cards or family book
  • Young: ID card or student card
  • Disabled: disability certificate
