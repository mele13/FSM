FSM is a company that offers fully equipped space to practice sport while being secure and feeling confident.
Each person has their own needs. We try to adapt to your needs and help you through your fitness journey.

Our main Gym, FSM Maspalomas is a multi-level facility that houses an expansive selection of equipment. All of our gyms pretend to imitate Maspalomas’ FSM as it’s one of the Canary Island’s VIP fitness center.

Whether your passion is cardio, weight training, bodybuilding, powerlifting, CrossFit, swimming or functional training; we have all the equipment and necessary machines you need to obtain results.

Make sure you also check all the activities and courses available at FSM.
